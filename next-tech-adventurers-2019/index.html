Try the delicious food!

Injera is a flatbread that is common in Ethiopia. Injera is made of teff, a grass that is cultivated for its edible seeds and is the main ingredient in Injera. Its eaten with a variety of stews and vegatables. It can be used with lamb, chicken, lentils and many other flavors.
